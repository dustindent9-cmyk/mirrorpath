Look in the Mirror

Stage: Questions

Each question is explained. Answer like you would if no one else would ever read it — because the system is built from your truth, not your performance.

1. Your name
What should the Mirror call you? First name is enough. This is how your daily speeches will address you.
2. Who are you right now — honestly?
Not your resume. Not who you pretend to be on social media. The plain truth of your current season — strengths, mess, and all. Example: “I’m a hard worker who keeps restarting and never finishing.”
3. Who must you become in the next 90 days?
This is your “better self” — the polished, disciplined man or woman you are building. Be specific. Example: “A man who wakes on time, keeps his word, trains his body, and leads his house with calm strength.”
4. What has stopped you until now?
Name the real blocker — fear, comfort, chaos, addiction to distraction, no plan, wrong friends, no accountability. If you won’t name it, it will keep running your life.
5. What is your current life costing you?
Cost can be money, health, respect, peace, family trust, or years. We need the price of staying the same so change feels worth it.
6. One habit to build. One habit to break.
Keep it small and daily. One build + one break beats ten vague goals. Example: “Build: morning journal. Break: late-night scrolling.”
7. Readiness (1–10)
1 = curious only. 10 = I will do the work even when I don’t feel like it. Be honest — the system adjusts tone to your readiness.
8. Which path matters most right now?
Your Life OS will open around this path first. You can use all paths later.
